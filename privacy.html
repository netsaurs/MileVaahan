Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, phone number, vehicle details), information collected automatically (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, location when permitted), and information from third parties (Google Sign-In, Razorpay payment data).

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide and improve our services, match buyers with suitable vehicles, send service updates and transactional emails, prevent fraud and ensure platform safety, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information with: verified dealers (only when you express interest), service providers (Firebase, Razorpay, cloud hosting), and law enforcement when legally required.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for functionality, analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4) to improve the platform, and preference cookies to remember your settings. You may opt out of analytics cookies at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3), Firebase Security Rules for database access control, and regular security audits. No system is 100% secure; we encourage strong passwords.

6. Your Rights

Under India's DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data. Contact privacy@milevaahan.com to exercise these rights. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Transaction records are kept for 7 years per Indian tax law. Analytics data is retained for 26 months.
